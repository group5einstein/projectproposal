INTRODUCTION

In the locality of Tagum City, residents nowadays are always busy. People are up as early as dawn and go home late as dusk. If they have free time, they would use that to rest instead. They will have little to no more time anymore to do little tasks like watering their plants or walking their dogs. As students, we would like to propose our project called “Chore Website”. A site where anyone can get little jobs with compensation. It is a free and accessible site where both parties, the boss and the job looker, can benefit. This project will surely help a lot of busy individuals such as teachers like Sir Ernie Balbuena and other teachers of Tagum City National High School. The goal of this project is to provide help to busy Tagumeños who need someone to do a job for them and to also help people to have an extra source of income.
